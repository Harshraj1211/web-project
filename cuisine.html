North Indian Cuisine


Noth Indian is known for its rice, aromatic, and flavorful dishes often cooked with ghee and spices:

  • Biryani:
    • A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spice and mear or vegetables.
  • Butter Chicken:
    • A creamy, tomato-based curry that's a favorite world wide.
  • Paneer Tikka
    • Marinated panner cubes grilled to perfection.
