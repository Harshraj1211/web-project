Iconic Monuments


India is home to some of the most awe-inspiring landmarks in the world. These monuments reflectits rich cultutal heritage and historical significance:

  • Taj Mahal:
    • located in agra, this symbol of eternal love is one of the Seven wonders of the World.
  • Red Fort:
    • A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi, Showcasing Mughal architecture.
  • Hawa Mahal:
    • Knows as the "Palace of winds,"this Jaipr marvel is famous for its intricate latticework.
  • Charminar:
    • An iconic Mosque in Hydrabad and a masterpiece of Indo- Islamic architecture.
